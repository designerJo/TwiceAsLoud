Welcome to the on-line newsletter of the Twice as Loud theatre company, formed in 1996 by students of Craig Snelling's Acting Studio at Morley College in London.
    Our members write, produce, direct and act in shows on the London fringe.
    At present we have no plans for any new shows during 2005.
