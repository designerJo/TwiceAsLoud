Stage Bites, Stage Bites Back and Fire in my Shorts

Our first project, an evening of short plays titled 'Stage Bites' ran for one week at the Landor pub in Clapham, London in February 1996.
    The sell out shows encouraged us to book the theatre for a further two weeks in July 1996 for 'Stage Bites Back'.
    The year was ended with a further week of short plays in December, the 'Fire in my shorts' show.
    In our first year of public shows we produced, directed and performed in twenty-nine short plays.
