Five shows, two prize winners and a wedding

A new show and a new venue for Twice as Loud to start our 1997 performances. 'A quick one upstairs' was another evening of six short plays, hosted by the Lion and Unicorn theatre pub in Kentish Town, North London.
    In June we put on four one act plays under the imaginative title 'Fourplay', back at the Landor in Clapham.
    'Straight Talking' was the first of our September double bill followed closely by 'Love Talking' the following week. Both prompting the question "will twice as loud ever do a show without an orgasm in it?"
    The last show of the year, 'First Exposure', featured new plays written by Twice as Loud members and participants in the monthly play reading evenings.

Westminster play writing prize winners
In the summer Suzy took first prize at the Westminster play writing festival with 'Waltzing's for dreamers" and Rachel took joint second prize with "Salt".
