The programme

Burning love
Scripted by: Suzy Almond and Chris Thomas
Actors: Gerard Fernandes as Filippo, Suzy Almond as Bernie, Robert Read as Steve, Sarah Craig as Vinnie and Sarah Milrose as Marion
Director: Chris Thomas

This piece has been devised and originated by Twice as Loud to complement the themes running through Effie's burning. The director created the piece through a series of improvisations over a period of two months.
    The five actors all created an individual character based a round a common theme of finding a place that they could call home. That home is a pub and the play starts as "The World's End" re-opens to its customers after a fire.

Effie's burning
Author: Valerie Windsor
Actors: Enid Gayle as Effie Palmer and Caroline Ross as Dr. Ruth Kovacs
Director: Claudette Jacobs

This is the story of an old lady released into the community after 50 years in a mental institution.
    The play is narrated by a young doctor who, through her growing friendship with Effie, learns to stand up for herself in a male dominated profession. In this play, fire is not purely a destructive force.
