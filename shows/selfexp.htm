selfexposure

8 new plays by 5 new writers

18th November to 5th December 1998 at the Landor pub theatre Clapham, London

The programme

Learning to swim
Author Peter Cunningham
Actors: Enid Gayle, Gunther Wurger, Patricia Gagni, Mark Jackson
Director: Diana Chappel

'If I could teach you, we could kill two birds with one stone'.

Someone's rocking my dreamboat
Author: Caroline Ross
Actors: Sarah Milrose, John Greenwood
Director: Chris Thomas

It's night, father sits in the garden waiting to greet aliens with a nip of whiskey and the incomparable voice of Al Bowley.

Important information
Author Peter Cunningham
Actors: Peter Cunningham, Sarah Nunn, Enid Gayle
Director: with the assistance of Patricia Gagni

Late one summer in the small hours Wessex and Eric are glued to the TV.

1,001 nights
Author Caroline Ross
Actors: Caroline Ross
Director: Sarah Craig

By the light of a lantern. The darkest fairytale of a woman in love with a sultan.

Meeting in the street
Author Peter Cunningham
Actors: Peter Cunningham, Anne Corcoran, Phil Jarvis, Gisa Wyrkow
Director: Enid Gayle

A street side cafe, Duncan bumps into Kate.

The man who couldn't swallow lies
Author Maureen Delenian
Actors: Sarah Nunn, Tyrone Atkins, Sarah Craig, Patricia Gagni, Mark Jackson
Director: Sarah Craig

Guilty as charged. A man with a taste for the truth defends himself with a simple demonstration.

Crazy little thing called love
Author Lesley Menzies
Actors: Sarah Milrose, Caroline Ross
Director: Chris Thomas

A beautiful garden outside a tea shop. Vicki and June rest their legs and eat cake.

Emma's last date
Author Hugo Simms
Actors: Sarah Craig, Rob Widdicombe
Director: Hugo Simms

'Do you like cheesecake, Emma?'

With thanks to:
Stage manager: Paul Cook
Sound: Patrick Dixon, Rob Widdicombe, Chris Thomas
Lighting: Sarah Doonican
