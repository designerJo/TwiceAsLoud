The 'First exposure' programme
20-6 December 1997
Upstairs at the Landor, Clapham, London

All the scripts are the work of new writers and were selected from the monthly play reading evenings.
Find details of future play readings on the news page

Up the Hill
"I've got a new fridge"
Author: Martin Cooper
Actors: Anne Corcoran, Maurice McParland and Ivan Dalmedo
Director: Claudette Jacobs

Flowing into the sun
1945. The backyard of a terraced house in Liverpool. Vinnie's dad has got something to say.
Author: Peter Cunningham
Actors: Peter Cunningham and Tyrone Atkins
Director: Claudette Jacobs
Light Fandango
A drab suburban sitting room, take-away food and gin, Marge and Denise enjoy a quiet night in.
Author: Lesley Menzies
Actors: Caroline Ross and Enid Gayle
Director: Peter Cunningham

Photo: Enid in Light Fandango
Salt
Joint second prize winner in the 1997 Westminster Prize run by the Soho Theatre.
"He would have smelt the salt in the air, wouldn't he?"
Author: Rachel Williams
Actors: Sarah Craig and Chris Thomas
Directors: Caroline Ross and Enid Gayle

Dexter's diary
The bold and the beautiful, the rich and the famous, and, er... Dexter.
Author: Suzy Almond
Actors: Chris Thomas, Sarah Milrose, Rob Widdecombe and Mark Jackson
Director: Chris Thomas

Bluebell Wood
Milo takes Kate for a walk in the woods.
Author: Hugo Simms
Actors: Gerard Fernandes and Liz Bentley
Director: Diana Chappell

Waltzing's for dreamers
First prize winner in the 1997 Westminster Prize run by the Soho Theatre.
"Play me a blue song and fade down the light,
I'm sad as a proud man can be sad tonight"
Author: Suzy Almond
Actors: Sarah Craig and Johnny Harris
Director: Suzy Almond
Beating around the bush
Deepest England, the other side of a tall hedge. Two strangers meet and discover shared interests.
Author: Peter Cunningham
Actors: Peter Cunningham and Caroline Ross
Director: Enid Gayle
