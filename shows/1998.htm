Twice as Loud: bold as brass
The February / March 1998 edition of "The guide to Beyond the West End" included a short feature on Twice as Loud. Based on an interview with founder members, Hugo Simms and Sarah Milrose, it outlines the company's history and is very encouraging about its future. The company is described as "a spirited and eclectic bunch of writers/performers who view the world as inquisitively as new-born babies."
    The guide to theatre, dance and exhibitions is distributed in fringe venues around London.
