Review from London Fringe+ magazine
The show was reviewed by Alastair Baker in the July 1998 edition of London Fringe + magazine:

    "The artistry and industry of Twice as Loud never ceases to amaze me... this double bill of human desire, both burning and vanquished, succeeds in wringing out your heart while laughing your socks off.
    Twice as Loud's ability, aside from having actors who can act, is in capturing the nuances of life so well."
