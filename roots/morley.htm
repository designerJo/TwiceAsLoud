Craig Snelling's Acting Studio at Morley College

Most of the cast members of Twice as Loud were formerly students of Craig Snelling who remains an inspirational artistic consultant to the company.
    Craig runs the 'Acting Studio' once a week at Morley College, an adult education college in Lambeth, south London. The Acting Studio participants often go on to stage public shows and several theatre companies have been formed as a result, the most recent being 'Third Eye' and 'Fourth Wall'.
    Morley College can be contacted by telephone on +44(0)171 928 8501

The creation of Twice as Loud
Out of necessity our end of course productions at Morley College were short plays lasting no more than 15 minutes each. We decided that the short play was an accessible and exciting format and one suited ideally to the fringe pub theatre circuit in London. We decided to use this as the basis for our first public productions.
