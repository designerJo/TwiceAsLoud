Morley College

The founder members of Twice as Loud met at Craig Snelling's 'Acting Studio' at Morley College. Their experiences there inspired them to go on and create their own theatre company which has since performed over 50 short plays in public.

more about the Acting Studio

 The name

The name, 'Twice as Loud', was the result of much heated debate and choosing it was one of the first tests of the company's decision making capabilities.

Why 'Twice as Loud'?
The name is a tribute to their teacher at Morley College, Craig Snelling, who, after each rehearsal or improvisation would say "Great, now once more and this time, twice as loud!".
