Craig Snelling
As well as being Twice as Loud's creative advisor, Craig is a teacher, musician, director and actor.

Teaching

 Craig develops and presents writing, acting and directing courses whose purpose is to provide students with practical skills, inspiration and confidence to work creatively in all these fields.
    It is this work which has led to the formation of independent theatre companies such as Twice as Loud and Third Eye.

Creative achievements

 1992 shortlisted for Buxton Festival of Musicals for his first Opera, 'Some contact'.

1992 Songwriter of Great Britain award.

Stage performances include work on the Lindsey Kemp production of Salome and the Krunchy Twins comedy cabaret.
