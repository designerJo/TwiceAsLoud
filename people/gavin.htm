January 1997:
Web site brings an American fan to Twice as Loud

(Sorry Gavin - the one with the bowler hat was out of focus!)

 This picture shows Gerard from Twice as Loud (left) with Gavin Osborne (right) from Louisville, USA, who discovered Twice as Loud through this very web site.
When Gavin planned a visit to London, UK, in January 1997 he emailed us first and arranged to sit in on one of our play reading evenings. Gavin helped us out with our American accents and we helped him to plenty of beer and a great time was had by all!
    So if you are planning a trip to London and want some friendly theatrical company please feel free to join us at one of our play reading evenings. (Email us if you want more information).
