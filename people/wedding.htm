Twice as Loud member breaks the hearts of millions

 
 
Anne Corcoran married Rob Leedham in Cambridge on the 6th September 1997.


A lifelong fan (camping outside the church with the thousands of others barred from the private ceremony) was heard to say "I'm totally gutted, they didn't even invite me to the party".
    The wedding was the social event of the year with more big hats than you could shake a stick at. The ceremony was absolutely lovely. The reception was held in one of Cambridge's original colleges, steeped in history and (by the end of the meal) wine.
    The day was rounded off with a traditional Irish Ceili which involved lots of mad rushing around in small sweaty circles and stamping on each others feet in time to music.
    Congratulations to the happy couple and commiserations to the disappointed fans, but cheer up - there are still six unmarried and immensely attractive women in Twice as Loud.
