I'm in my last year at college studying theatre (or a related subject) and I'm looking for work when I graduate. Can you help?

Twice as Loud exists to provide experience for its members and exposure on the London fringe theatre circuit. We are not in a position to pay anyone who is involved with the company. However, we may be able to offer some experience.
    In the past we have auditioned for roles that we cannot fill ourselves and we regularly use people from outside of the company to help us with stage management, lighting and sound.
    If you are interested then come and meet us at one of our shows or play reading evenings. Alternatively, you can write to us.
