Can I send you my new play?
What kind of scripts are you interested in?

We welcome any scripts (finished or unfinished) which meet the following criteria:

  • Less than 20 minutes in length.
  • Preferably written for stage (not radio or TV)
  • Written in English
  • Original and, preferably, new to the UK

Before you send a script please take note of the following points:

  • Please send plays by post as email is rarely successful.
  • We cannot promise to return scripts so please do not send originals.
  • If you require feedback on your script please provide an email or postal address with the script.
  • If you live in London then you will get a more immediate response if you bring your script along to one of our  play reading evenings in person.

Note that our feedback tends to be critical. All our comments are intended to be constructive.

Unfortunately, it is not always possible to give feedback on all the plays that are sent to us.
