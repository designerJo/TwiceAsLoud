My theatre company is thinking of setting up a web site. Can you offer any advice?

Some professional input helps make the site attractive, functional and as fast as possible (an important consideration when producing a web site). Ask around to see if you have a friend with the relevant experience to help you out. If there is no-one with experience then try to find an enthusiastic computer literate graphic designer who is willing to learn about HTML and could be persuaded to use your site as a test project.
    Note that both design and technical skills are important factors in creating a successful site. If you have difficulty finding them, contact our site designer, Joanna Bennett who is a trained graphic designer with experience of designing for the web. She can give you an idea of what is involved and may be able to get you started (at a reasonable charge, of course!).
