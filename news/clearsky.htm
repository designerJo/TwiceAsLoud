Now performing:
Hoping for a clear sky

Several members of Twice as Loud are involved in the current production from the Third Eye Theatre Company.
    Like Twice as Loud, Third Eye evolved from Craig Snelling's Acting Studio at Morley College.

The programme
Eight short plays plotting a dark and humorous journey. An exploration of hope, despair, joy, desperation, madness and the ultimate triumph of the spirit.
    From aliens to bedside manners, drumming to silence, domination to supplication...
    New writing, new dimension, new vision.
