Monday 10th January: next new play reading evening

Writers are invited to submit scripts for the new play reading evenings. Guests are welcome to bring a script in person or come along to give feedback on the plays being read.
    At the play readings you can meet writers, directors and actors from Twice as Loud, Third Eye and other theatre companies and writing groups.

The objective
 The play reading evenings provide encouragement and feedback for new writers in a friendly environment.

Venue:
Upstairs at the Crown and Cushion pub, 133, Westminster Bridge Road, London,
SE1
UK

Nearest tubes:
Lambeth North and Waterloo.

Time:
7.30 to 10.00 p.m.

Public performances
 With the writers' consent, theatre companies will select scripts from these readings to perform at future shows. Peter Cunningham's 'Beating around the Bush' was first read at our play reading evening and will form part of the Union Theatre's December show. The 'First Exposure' and 'Self Exposure' shows both featured new scripts selected by Twice as Loud from the play reading evenings.

Photo: Sarah Craig in Suzy Almond's Waltzing's for Dreamers, first performed as part of the First Exposure show

Award winning plays
 Two of the writers who aired their scripts at the play reading evenings went on to enter them into the 1997 Westminster Play reading Competition. They  won first and second prizes respectively!
